# SaaS Billing & Inventory Management System - PRD

## Core Screens to Generate:
1. **Login Page**: Clean, professional entry point with brand logo and JWT-ready fields.
2. **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive overview with sales graphs (Chart.js style), revenue stats, and stock alerts.
3. **POS Billing Interface**: Optimized Point of Sale with product search, cart, and multi-payment options.
4. **Product Management**: Data table with filtering, search, and "Add Product" functionality.
5. **Inventory Management**: Stock tracking, low-stock alerts, and audit logs.
6. **Customer Management**: Profile views with purchase history and loyalty points.
7. **Reports Module**: Visual data summaries for sales, GST, and P&L.
8. **Settings & AI Analytics**: Business config and predictive trends.

## Tech Stack (UI Representation):
- React + Tailwind CSS
- Chart.js for data visualization
- Glassmorphism effects
- Sidebar-based navigation

## User Roles:
- Admin (Full Access)
- Manager (Inventory, Customers, Reports)
- Cashier (Billing only)
